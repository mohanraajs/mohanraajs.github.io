Asteroid Dodger is a completely offline game that does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Information We Collect

Asteroid Dodger is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Contact information
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Network or internet activity

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Game Progress: Your unlocked rockets, upgrade levels, and high scores
  • Game Settings: Your preferred asteroid settings (size, speed, shape)
  • Star Points: In-game currency earned through gameplay

Important: All game data is stored locally using SQLite database on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

3. Internet Access

Asteroid Dodger is a completely offline game. The app:

  • Does not require an internet connection to play
  • Does not communicate with any external servers
  • Does not send any data over the internet
  • Does not download additional content

4. Third-Party Services

Asteroid Dodger does NOT integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Payment processors

5. Children's Privacy

Asteroid Dodger is safe for children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):

  • The app is designed to be family-friendly
  • No personal information is collected from users of any age
  • No communication features that could expose children to contact with strangers
  • No inappropriate content or advertisements

6. Permissions

The app may request the following minimal permissions:

  • Storage Access: Only to save game progress locally on your device
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback during gameplay (if applicable)

No other permissions are requested or required.

7. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted:
